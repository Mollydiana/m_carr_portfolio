A bit little about me:

 
{#
That's cool, but sit tight; let's break the ice a little.
#}

I have always been fascinated with the interface between language and community . I have taught English in Spain and Spanish in the U.S., earned a B.A. in social science, worked with refugees from Nepal, Congo, Ethiopia and Mexico, and played traditional music on stages and at festivals in the U.S. and Galicia.
In continuing my focus on social/entrepreneurial endeavors, this past year I have concentrated on learning front-end development. I have come to realize that programming is the nexus of social connectivity, language and technology, and learning to be a good programmer/designer is my current objective.

 

I just recently finished an intensive 12-Week full stack web development immersion program at RocketU. During my time there I discovered that I have a passion and a knack for creating beautiful, simple, intuitive and user-friendly interfaces.

 

  • Python/Django
  • Angular/Javascript
  • UI/UX/Usability
